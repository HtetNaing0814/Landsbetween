THE GOLDEN ORDER
GLOBAL SUMMIT 2022
The Global Summit brings together the community of prophets, astrologers, samurais, prisoners, warriors and vegabonds who comprise the Golden Order affiliate network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on multiplayer summons that advance the cause of the Shattering, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the battles of open content and knowledge-free carian advocates, black flame monks, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, the orders and foundations, banished knights, and activists-we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.
Please contact us per Email for any further questions about The Golden Order Global Summit 2022!
